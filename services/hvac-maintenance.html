Comprehensive HVAC Maintenance Plans in Castroville, TX

Regular HVAC maintenance is the key to reliable performance, energy efficiency, and equipment longevity. Our comprehensive HVAC maintenance plans in Castroville, TX, provide scheduled service that keeps your heating and cooling systems operating at peak performance while preventing costly breakdowns and extending equipment life. Preventive maintenance is far more cost-effective than reactive repairs. Our maintenance plan customers experience significantly fewer emergency breakdowns, enjoy lower energy costs, and benefit from extended equipment warranties. With rising energy costs and increasing equipment complexity, professional maintenance has never been more important for Castroville homeowners.

Comprehensive Maintenance Services

Our HVAC maintenance plans include thorough inspection, cleaning, and optimization of all system components. We service all types of heating and cooling equipment including central air systems, furnaces, heat pumps, boilers, and ductless mini split systems. Each maintenance visit includes detailed documentation of system condition and recommendations for any needed improvements.

Heating System Maintenance

Our heating maintenance includes thorough inspection of heat exchangers, testing of safety controls, cleaning of burner assemblies, lubrication of moving parts, and optimization of combustion efficiency. We also check and clean filters, inspect electrical connections, test thermostat operation, and verify proper venting and ventilation.

Cooling System Maintenance

Air conditioning maintenance includes cleaning of indoor and outdoor coils, checking refrigerant levels, inspecting electrical connections, testing capacitors and contactors, cleaning or replacing filters, and verifying proper drainage. We also test system performance and efficiency to ensure optimal cooling capacity and energy efficiency.

Seasonal Maintenance Services

Our maintenance programs include seasonal preparation services that ensure your HVAC systems are ready for peak operating seasons. Spring air conditioning preparation includes system startup, performance testing, and optimization for summer operation. Fall heating preparation includes safety inspections, efficiency testing, and system optimization for winter operation.

Spring AC Preparation

Spring maintenance prepares your air conditioning system for Texas's hot summer months. We clean coils, check refrigerant levels, test electrical components, verify proper drainage, and optimize system performance to ensure reliable cooling when temperatures rise.

Fall Heating Preparation

Fall heating maintenance ensures your heating system is safe and efficient for winter operation. We inspect heat exchangers, test safety controls, clean components, check venting systems, and verify proper combustion to ensure safe, reliable heating throughout the cold season.

Our ductless maintenance includes cleaning of indoor unit filters and coils, inspection of outdoor units, checking refrigerant lines for leaks, testing remote control and zone control functions, and verifying proper drainage and operation.
