Common HVAC Problems We Repair

Refrigerant Leaks and Issues

Refrigerant leaks are among the most common AC problems we encounter in Castroville. Low refrigerant levels can cause poor cooling performance, frozen coils, and compressor damage if not addressed promptly. Our technicians use electronic leak detectors and other specialized equipment to locate and repair refrigerant leaks, then properly recharge the system to manufacturer specifications.

Electrical Problems

HVAC systems rely on complex electrical components including contactors, capacitors, relays, and control boards. Electrical problems can cause complete system failures or intermittent operation issues. Our electrically trained technicians can diagnose and repair all types of electrical problems safely and efficiently.

Mechanical Component Failures

Mechanical components like blower motors, compressors, fans, and pumps can fail due to normal wear, lack of maintenance, or electrical issues. We maintain extensive inventories of replacement parts for major brands, allowing us to complete most mechanical repairs promptly.

Thermostat and Control Issues

Thermostat problems can cause a wide range of HVAC issues including temperature control problems, frequent cycling, and complete system failures. We service and repair all types of thermostats from basic mechanical units to advanced programmable and smart thermostats.

Preventive Maintenance and Repair Prevention

While we're always ready to provide expert repair services, we also believe in helping our customers prevent problems before they occur. Regular maintenance is the best way to avoid unexpected breakdowns and extend the life of your HVAC equipment.

Our comprehensive maintenance programs include thorough system inspections, cleaning, lubrication, and adjustment of all major components. We also identify potential problems early when they're easier and less expensive to repair. Many of our maintenance customers experience significantly fewer emergency repair calls and enjoy lower overall HVAC costs.
